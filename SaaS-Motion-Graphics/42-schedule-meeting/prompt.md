# Prompt — 42-schedule-meeting

The original prompt used to generate this motion graphic:

Scheduling a Meeting
Duration: 16 seconds

A calendar tool fills the frame — week view, Monday through Friday, business hours visible. The week is moderately busy — several blocks of time occupied across different days. A cursor clicks "Schedule Meeting" in the top right. A smart scheduling panel slides in from the right — an input for meeting title, duration selector, and an attendee field. The cursor types a meeting title. Selects 45 minutes from the duration dropdown. Types three attendee email addresses.
The panel processes — a brief loading shimmer — then displays three suggested time slots, each showing the day, time, and a green "All available" label. The calendar on the left simultaneously highlights the three suggested slots in soft blue, showing exactly where they land in the week. The cursor clicks the second suggestion. The meeting block appears on the calendar instantly — dropping into the chosen slot with a spring bounce, the block color matching the meeting type. A "Invites sent" toast appears in the top right. The panel closes. The calendar holds — one new block in the week, owned and scheduled.
