# Prompt — 43-live-chat-support

The original prompt used to generate this motion graphic:

Live Chat Support
Duration: 16 seconds

A customer support tool fills the frame — an inbox of open conversations on the left, the active conversation on the right. The active conversation is a customer asking a question — their message visible in a blue bubble. The support agent's response area is empty, cursor blinking.
A cursor clicks an AI Suggest button above the response field. A suggested reply types itself into the field — a full, well-written response, addressing the customer's question specifically. The cursor reads it — scrolling through the suggestion. Clicks Edit. Changes two words in the middle of the response — deleting and retyping, personalizing it. The cursor clicks Send. The message appears in the conversation as a gray bubble — sent, delivered, readable.
The customer's status indicator in the conversation header switches from "Reading" to "Typing." Three dots appear in the conversation. A reply appears — a short thank you. The cursor marks the conversation as Resolved. It moves to the top of the left inbox, its status badge switching from Open to Resolved in green. The next conversation in the inbox highlights — the cursor is already moving toward it.
