# Prompt — 24-headline-morph

The original prompt used to generate this motion graphic:

Duration: 14 seconds

White canvas. A full product headline sits centered — SF Pro Display Heavy, 72px, three words. It holds for 2 seconds. Then the third word blurs out — a fast defocus, 6 frames — and a new word blurs in at the same position, replacing it. The sentence has changed its meaning. Holds 2 seconds. The second word morphs — same blur mechanic. New sentence. Holds. The first word morphs. The headline has completely transformed across three morphs, each swap changing the meaning of the whole line. On the fourth and final version, no morph follows. The headline holds. The canvas background very slowly warms from pure white toward a barely perceptible cream over the remaining 3 seconds. The type stays black. The warmth is the only thing that moves.
