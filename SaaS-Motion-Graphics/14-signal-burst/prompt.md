# Prompt — 14-signal-burst

The original prompt used to generate this motion graphic:

A black circular hub node appears at the exact center of a light gray canvas. It springs in with a single confident pop — no wobble, one overshoot, clean settle.
Eight thin lines radiate outward from the hub simultaneously, growing toward eight satellite nodes arranged in a perfect circle around it. The lines draw at identical speed, creating a perfect starburst extension. As the lines finish extending, all eight satellite nodes pop in simultaneously — same spring timing as the hub.
A beat of stillness. Then eight small blue dots, one per connector line, begin traveling outward from the hub toward their respective satellites. They move in a continuous loop — reaching the satellite, instantly resetting to the hub, traveling again. Each dot is offset slightly in its loop phase so no two dots are at the same position simultaneously. The effect reads as a gentle, constant pulse of outward energy — like signals being broadcast. It loops indefinitely with no change in speed or rhythm.
