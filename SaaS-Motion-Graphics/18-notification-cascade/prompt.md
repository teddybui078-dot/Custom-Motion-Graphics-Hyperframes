# Prompt — 18-notification-cascade

The original prompt used to generate this motion graphic:

A product dashboard sits in a browser frame on white. Everything is calm and static. Then a notification badge appears on a bell icon in the top nav — a small red dot, hard cut, no animation. Then another badge appears on a sidebar item. Then a toast slides in from the top right — a single line of text, white card, faint shadow, spring entrance. Then a second toast stacks below it. Then a third. They arrive at irregular intervals, the way real notifications do — sometimes close together, sometimes with a gap. The dashboard beneath them is still perfectly calm. The world is happening outside the product. The product is just showing the user where to look.
