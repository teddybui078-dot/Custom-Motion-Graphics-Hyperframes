# Prompt — 32-task-detail-panel

The original prompt used to generate this motion graphic:

Duration: 18 seconds
A project board fills the frame — three columns, a mix of task cards across them. A cursor clicks on one specific card — it opens into a detail panel sliding in from the right, taking up the right third of the screen. The task detail panel shows a title, description, status dropdown, assignee field, due date, priority selector, and a comment thread below.
The cursor clicks the assignee field — a dropdown opens showing team member avatars and names. The cursor scrolls through the list and clicks one name. The avatar appears in the assignee field, the dropdown closes. The cursor clicks the due date field — a calendar appears. The cursor clicks a date 5 days out. The calendar closes, the date populates. The cursor clicks the priority selector — High is selected from a dropdown, the field turns amber. The cursor clicks into the description field and types two sentences — fast, purposeful.
The cursor clicks outside the panel. It slides closed. The task card on the board has updated — the assignee avatar is now visible on the card, the due date shown, a small amber priority dot in the corner. The card looks owned. The task has a person, a deadline, and a priority. The cursor moves away.
