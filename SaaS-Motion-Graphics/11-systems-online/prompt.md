# Prompt — 11-systems-online

The original prompt used to generate this motion graphic:

On a deep dark navy canvas, five horizontal bars materialize from bottom to top, one at a time. Each bar slides in from slightly off to the left and fades in simultaneously — not a dramatic entrance, just a clean, confident slide. They arrive in sequence with a tight stagger, like cards being dealt onto a table. By the time the last bar lands, the stack feels solid and architectural.
A faint horizontal light sweeps downward across the full stack — barely visible, like a scanner passing over the surface. It moves at a steady linear pace from top to bottom and disappears.
In its wake, each bar's status badge switches from a dim neutral state to a vivid green "Active" state, one by one from top to bottom, with a tight stagger. The switch is instantaneous per badge — no fade, no animation on the badge itself, just a hard cut from off to on. The sequence reads like systems coming online.
After the last badge activates, all five bars enter a slow breathing loop — their opacity rises and falls gently, each bar slightly out of phase with the one above it. The ripple moves top to bottom, continuously, like a slow wave passing through the stack.
