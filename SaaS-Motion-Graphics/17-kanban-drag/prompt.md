# Prompt — 17-kanban-drag

The original prompt used to generate this motion graphic:

A kanban board fills the frame — three columns, To Do, In Progress, Done. Each column has cards. A cursor hovers over one card in To Do. The card lifts — scale increases slightly, shadow deepens, it separates from the column visually. The cursor drags it rightward across the board, the card tracking with a 4-frame lag behind the cursor, natural and physical. It crosses into In Progress. A blue insertion indicator appears between two existing cards — a thin horizontal line showing exactly where it will land. The cursor releases. The card drops into place with a single satisfying bounce. The column reorders itself around it. The cursor moves away. The board holds.
