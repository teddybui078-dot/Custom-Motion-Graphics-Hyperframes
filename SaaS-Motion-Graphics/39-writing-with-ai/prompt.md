# Prompt — 39-writing-with-ai

The original prompt used to generate this motion graphic:

Writing with AI
Duration: 20 seconds

A document editor fills the frame — a long-form writing tool, a blank document open, just a blinking cursor on the first line. A title types itself in — slow, deliberate, the way someone writes a headline they're not sure about yet. Below the title, two sentences of body copy type in. Then the typing stops. The cursor sits for 2 seconds — thinking.
The cursor selects the two sentences — a click and drag highlight. Clicks a small AI button that appears above the selection — a sparkle icon, subtle. A dropdown appears: Expand, Rewrite, Make shorter, Change tone. The cursor clicks Expand. The two sentences blur slightly — a defocus lasting 4 frames — then resolve into six sentences, the original ideas developed and extended, the new text appearing as if typed at high speed. The expansion settles. The cursor clicks outside the selection. The document now has a proper opening paragraph.
The cursor clicks at the end of the paragraph and starts typing a new sentence — gets three words in and stops. An autocomplete suggestion appears in light gray extending the sentence. The cursor hits Tab. The suggestion accepts — the gray text turns black, the sentence completes itself. The cursor continues typing the next sentence. The document is being written faster than one person could write it alone.
